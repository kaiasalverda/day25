Artist Statement

I am doing my project about lacrosse. I chose to do this because lacrosse is something that is very important to me, and not a very well-known sport so I thought it would be interesting to document. I think if I capture a lot of different aspects of the sport it will shine some light on something that isnâ€™t very main stream. My goal for this is to shine a new light on the sport and to give people insight to something they might not know a lot about. For this I took pictures of different lacrosse events I went to. One of the sets of pictures is from an indoor tournament called 3x lacrosse. I thought this would be interesting because it will give some contrast to the pictures that I take outside. I also am planning on taking some photos of someone just messing around with a lacrosse stick. I want to try to capture the different aspects of lacrosse and show how in some peoples lives it is almost always present.
